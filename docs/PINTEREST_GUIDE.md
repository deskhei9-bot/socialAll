# Pinterest User Guide

This guide explains how to connect and use your Pinterest account with Social Symphony.

## Connecting Your Pinterest Account

1.  **Go to Channels**: Navigate to the "Channels" page from the main menu.
2.  **Find Pinterest**: Locate the Pinterest platform in the list of available connections.
3.  **Click Connect**: Click the "Connect" button next to the Pinterest logo.
4.  **Authorize Access**: You will be redirected to Pinterest to authorize Social Symphony to access your account.
5.  **Redirect Back**: After successful authorization, you will be redirected back to the Channels page.

Your Pinterest account is now connected!

## Creating a Pin

1.  **Go to Create Post**: Navigate to the "Create Post" page.
2.  **Select Pinterest**: Select the Pinterest icon from the platform selector.
3.  **Choose Post Type**: Select "Pin (Image)" as the post type.
4.  **Upload an Image**: Upload the image you want to pin.
5.  **Add Title and Description**: Fill in the title and description for your pin.
6.  **Select a Board**: Choose the board you want to post your pin to.
7.  **Publish or Schedule**: Click "Publish Now" or schedule your pin for a later time.

Your pin will be published to your selected Pinterest board.
