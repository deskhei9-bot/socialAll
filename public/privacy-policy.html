Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SocialFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social media management platform.

Important: By using SocialFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Avatar/profile picture, bio, preferences
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications

2.2 Social Media Account Information

When you connect your social media accounts to SocialFlow, we collect:

  • Facebook/Instagram: Page IDs, page names, access tokens, page follower counts
  • YouTube: Channel IDs, channel names, access tokens, subscriber counts
  • TikTok: Account IDs, usernames, access tokens, follower counts
  • Twitter/X: Account IDs, usernames, access tokens
  • LinkedIn: Profile/page IDs, names, access tokens
  • Telegram: Bot tokens, channel information
  • Pinterest: Board IDs, account information, access tokens

2.3 Content Information

  • Posts, captions, and hashtags you create
  • Media files (images, videos) you upload
  • Scheduling information for posts
  • Templates and saved content

2.4 Usage Information

  • Activity logs and timestamps
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error logs and debugging information

2.5 Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate and authorize your access
  • Connect and manage your social media accounts
  • Publish posts to your connected platforms
  • Schedule and automate content distribution
  • Store and manage your media files

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Monitor and analyze platform performance
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3.3 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important updates about our service
  • Provide customer support

3.4 AI Features

  • Generate captions and content suggestions using OpenAI API
  • Provide hashtag recommendations
  • Optimize content for different platforms

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Database: PostgreSQL 16 with encrypted connections
  • Media Files: Stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Backups: Regular automated backups for data recovery
  • Location: Data stored in secure data centers

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted password storage using bcrypt
  • JWT tokens for secure authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and permission management
  • Database triggers for data validation

4.3 Access Tokens

Social media access tokens are stored securely and encrypted. We only use these tokens to perform actions you explicitly request (posting, scheduling, etc.). Tokens are never shared with third parties.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party services:

  • Social Media Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, Pinterest - to publish your content
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered caption and hashtag generation (only content you choose to generate)
  • Cloud Storage: For media file storage and delivery

5.2 We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Analytics companies (beyond anonymous usage statistics)
  • Any other third parties for their marketing purposes

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Government requests
  • Protection of our rights and safety
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

6. Your Data Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

  • View all your personal data in your account settings
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Download your content and media files

6.2 Modification and Correction

  • Update your profile information anytime
  • Correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Modify your preferences and settings

6.3 Deletion Rights

  • Delete individual posts and content
  • Remove connected social media accounts
