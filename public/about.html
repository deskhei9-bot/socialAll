Our Story

SocialFlow was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to give content creators and businesses complete control over their social media management without depending on expensive third-party SaaS platforms or compromising data privacy.

In an era where social media presence is crucial for success, we recognized the challenges faced by creators, agencies, and businesses managing multiple platforms. Traditional solutions were either too expensive, lacked essential features, or required trusting sensitive data to external services.

We built SocialFlow as a 100% self-hosted solution that puts you in complete control. Your data stays on your server, your content remains yours, and you have the freedom to customize and scale as needed.

Our Technology

Built with modern, reliable, and scalable technology:

Frontend

  • React 18 with TypeScript for type-safe, maintainable code
  • Vite for lightning-fast development and building
  • shadcn-ui for beautiful, accessible components
  • Tailwind CSS for responsive, mobile-first design

Backend

  • Node.js with Express for robust API server
  • PostgreSQL 16 for reliable data storage
  • PM2 for process management and auto-restart
  • JWT authentication for secure access

Infrastructure

  • Nginx for reverse proxy and SSL termination
  • Docker support for easy deployment
  • Automated backups for data safety
  • Monitoring and logging for reliability
